\# Whole Body \& Soul



\## Transformative Wellness Workshops in Ireland



Whole Body \& Soul offers holistic 90-minute wellness workshops that integrate movement, sound healing, shadow work, stretching, meditation, and ceremonial cacao to nurture complete well-being.



\## About Our Workshops



Our workshops provide a safe, supportive space for personal transformation through:



\- \*\*Mindful Movement\*\*: Gentle, intuitive practices to awaken the body

\- \*\*Sound Bath Healing\*\*: Therapeutic vibrations for deep relaxation

\- \*\*Shadow Work\*\*: Guided exploration of the inner landscape

\- \*\*Restorative Stretching\*\*: Increasing flexibility and body awareness

\- \*\*Guided Meditation\*\*: Cultivating inner peace and presence

\- \*\*Sacred Cacao Ceremony\*\*: Heart-opening plant medicine experience



\## Workshop Format



\*\*Duration\*\*: 90 minutes  

\*\*Location\*\*: Ireland  

\*\*Approach\*\*: Holistic integration of body, mind, and soul practices  

\*\*Experience Level\*\*: All welcome, no prior experience necessary
